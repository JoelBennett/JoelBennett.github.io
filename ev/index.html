Welcome to the EV calculator!

The purpose of this site is to determine the point in time at which switching over to an EV (electric vehicle) makes economic sense.

This will only give an approximate date, and it make make sense to switch to an EV sooner (or later) for other reasons.

Disclaimer:

>

No calculator will be perfect. This calculator makes a number of assumptions:

  • EV maintenance costs are significantly lower than internal combustion engines, but still not entirely $0. For example, brake pad replacements on the Nissan Leaf are much less frequent because of regenerative braking, which doesn't use the brake pads.
  • Some businesses and employers offer free charging. This may also reduce energy costs to the EV owner.
